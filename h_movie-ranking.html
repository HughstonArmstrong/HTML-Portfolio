Most MARVELous Movies and TV Shows for Hugh

Motivating Trio

Captain America: The First Avenger

Captain America established himself, not as superhuman, but super-stubborn. His commitment to the core values of to serve and protect and not backing down to a bully places Steve Rogers in a unique position to be the star spangled man with a plan. It's not about betting on the little guy, but betting on the biggest heart.

Avengers: Age of Ultron

James Spader with one of my favorite trailer lines "...there are no strings on me..." brings about an incredibe antagonist in the form of Ultron, a creation of Tony Stark's that takes a misguided, "updated," version of justice and safety for mankind that almost brings about it's extinction. The character development and challenge for the Avengers to come together to succeed brings a mission to protect a community, and the world.

Avengers: Endgame

The finality of this film for Phase 3 of MCU, the stage, the action, the effects, it all comes together in this showdown where there's only 1 in over 14 million chance.
